Jollof Rice

Prep Time: 15 mins | Cook Time: 45 mins | Total Time: 1 hr

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups long-grain parboiled rice
  • 5 tomatoes, 2 red bell peppers, 2 scotch bonnets
  • 1 large onion (half blended, half sliced)
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • Vegetable oil
  • 2 cups chicken stock
  • Curry, thyme, seasoning cubes, bay leaf, salt
  • 1 tbsp butter (optional)

Steps:

  1. Blend tomatoes, peppers and scotch bonnets into a smooth puree.
  2. Heat oil, fry sliced onions, then add tomato paste and fry 5 mins.
  3. Add blended mixture and fry 10–15 mins until oil separates.
  4. Add chicken stock, curry, thyme, seasoning cubes, and bay leaf.
  5. Wash rice and add to pot.
  6. Stir, cover tightly, cook on low heat.
  7. Stir once, add butter, cover and steam 10–15 mins.

Stewed Chicken

Prep Time: 20 mins | Cook Time: 40 mins | Total Time: 1 hr

Ingredients:

  • 1kg chicken pieces
  • Tomato stew base (blended tomatoes, peppers & onions)
  • Garlic, ginger
  • Seasoning cubes, curry, thyme, bay leaf

Steps:

  1. Season chicken with garlic, ginger, curry, thyme, salt and onions.
  2. Boil chicken until tender, reserve stock.
  3. Heat oil and fry tomato stew base 15 mins.
  4. Add chicken stock and seasoning.
  5. Add chicken pieces, simmer 10 mins.

Fried Rice

Prep Time: 15 mins | Cook Time: 30 mins | Total Time: 45 mins

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups rice
  • Mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, sweetcorn)
  • Diced chicken or liver
  • Soy sauce, curry, seasoning cubes

Steps:

  1. Parboil rice and cook halfway.
  2. Stir-fry vegetables and meat.
  3. Add soy sauce and seasoning.
  4. Add rice and mix thoroughly, steam 5 mins.

Roasted Chicken Skewers

Ingredients:

  • Diced chicken breast
  • Skewers
  • Paprika, garlic powder, black pepper, oil

Steps:

  1. Season chicken and marinate for 30 minutes.
  2. Thread chicken onto skewers.
  3. Roast or grill for 20–30 minutes, turning occasionally.

Pancakes

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup flour, 1 egg
  • ½ cup milk
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • Butter

Steps:

  1. Mix flour, sugar, milk and egg into smooth batter.
  2. Heat butter in a pan.
  3. Pour a scoop of batter, fry both sides until golden brown.

Egusi (Melon) Soup

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups blended melon seeds
  • Palm oil
  • Ugu or spinach
  • Beef, fish, stockfish
  • Crayfish, seasoning cubes

Steps:

  1. Heat palm oil, add blended melon and fry for 5–7 minutes.
  2. Add stock and stir until thick lumps form.
  3. Add meat, fish and crayfish.
  4. Add vegetables and simmer 5 minutes.
